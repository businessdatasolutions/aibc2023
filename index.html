Challenges

Quality monitoring:

  • Real-time data from servo motors, sensors, air, etc., is used to monitor product quality without the need for a camera system. For example, detecting misaligned flaps, products that are partially protruding, crooked or dented cartons, etc.

  • Combining data from various sources such as service data, Industry 4.0 machine measurement data, field and floor technicians’ knowledge, and supplier data to optimize maintenance schedules and prevent equipment failures (predictive maintenance)

Process improvements:

  • Analyze project data, costs, hours, delivery dates, etc., to identify opportunities for reducing lead times and costs.

  • Increase eficiency of machine pre-deployment testing through test automation.

Product enhancements:

  • Utilizing algorithms to explore multiple possibilities for product positioning.

  • Adjusting the control system based on real-time data. - When products enter the transport belt randomly, using algorithms to determine the most efficient formation for optimal throughput.
